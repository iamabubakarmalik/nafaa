## 📖 Full Description (Max 4000 characters)
Nafaa — Pakistan ka sab se asaan POS app for kiryana, medical, mobile shops, bakery, hardware, garments, and retail businesses.

Apni dukan ko smart tareeqe se chalayein. Sales, inventory, customers, khata (udhaar), aur reports — sab kuch ek hi app mein. Made by Pakistanis, for Pakistanis. 🇵🇰

✨ MAIN FEATURES

💰 POS BILLING — Sale Kareen Seconds Mein • Lightning-fast sale entry with auto-calculations • Built-in barcode scanner (no extra device needed) • Multiple payment methods: Cash, Card, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, Bank Transfer • Walk-in customers + registered customers support • Instant receipt generation with thermal printer support • Discount and tax settings (GST configurable) • Multi-line items with quantity, unit price, totals

📦 INVENTORY MANAGEMENT • Add unlimited products with photos • Categories, brands, and tags • Stock tracking with low-stock alerts • Product variants (size, color, etc.) • Multi-shop inventory (track stock per location) • Barcode-based product lookup • Bulk product import via CSV

👥 CUSTOMERS & KHATA (UDHAAR) • Customer database with phone, address, CNIC • Track udhaar (credit) per customer • Record payments and view ledger history • Send WhatsApp receipts (one-click) • Credit limit alerts • Top spenders & VIP customer reports

💸 EXPENSES & PURCHASES • Track daily/monthly expenses • Supplier management • Purchase orders with cost tracking • Stock adjustments and transfers • Returns management

📊 REPORTS & ANALYTICS • Daily, weekly, monthly sales reports • Profit & loss analysis • Top-selling products • Cashier performance reports • Category breakdown charts • Payment method analytics • Export to CSV/PDF

🌐 PAKISTANI-FIRST DESIGN • Full Urdu language support (اردو) • Right-to-left (RTL) layout • PKR currency by default • Local payment methods (JazzCash, EasyPaisa) • Hijri + Gregorian calendar • Roman Urdu friendly interface

🎨 BEAUTIFUL & MODERN UI • Dark mode + Light mode • Smooth animations • Easy-to-use design for shopkeepers • Works on all phone sizes • Offline support for sales (sync when online)

🔒 SECURE & RELIABLE • Bank-level encryption • Biometric login (Fingerprint/Face) • Multi-user role-based access • Automatic cloud backups • Two-factor authentication • Activity log for audit

🏪 PERFECT FOR ✓ Kiryana stores (general stores) ✓ Medical stores / pharmacies ✓ Mobile shops ✓ Bakeries ✓ Hardware stores ✓ Garment shops ✓ Cosmetic stores ✓ Stationery shops ✓ Restaurants / dhabas ✓ Any retail business

💎 WHY NAFAA?

Hum samajhte hain Pakistani shopkeepers ki zarooratein. Pakistan ka aik bohot bara dukandaar abhi tak khata register se kaam chalata hai. Sales ka hisaab, udhaar, products track karna — sab manual. Time waste hota hai, paisa khoata hai.

Nafaa solve karta hai yeh sab problems. Modern technology, simple language, Pakistani context. Khata register ko digital banayein, time bachayein, paisa kamayein.
