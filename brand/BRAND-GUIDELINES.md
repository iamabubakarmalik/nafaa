# Nafaa Brand Guidelines

## Logo Usage

### Variants Available
- `nafaa-logo-full.svg` — Icon + wordmark (primary)
- `nafaa-icon.svg` — Icon only (square, app icons, favicons)
- `nafaa-icon-dark.svg` — Icon for dark backgrounds
- `nafaa-wordmark.svg` — Wordmark only (no icon)
- `nafaa-urdu.svg` — With Urdu نفع text
- `nafaa-icon-mono-white.svg` — Single color (white bg)
- `nafaa-icon-mono-black.svg` — Single color (black bg)

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to 25% of logo height around all sides.

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 32px
- Print: 12mm

### Don'ts
- ❌ Don't stretch or distort
- ❌ Don't change colors outside brand palette
- ❌ Don't add effects (shadows other than approved, glows)
- ❌ Don't rotate
- ❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds

## Color Palette

### Primary
- **Brand Green:** `#16a34a` — Main brand color
- **Brand Green Dark:** `#15803d` — Hover/active states
- **Brand Green Light:** `#22c55e` — Light variants

### Accent
- **Amber:** `#f59e0b` — Highlights, premium features
- **Amber Light:** `#fbbf24` — Hover states

### Neutrals
- **Slate 950:** `#020617` — Primary text (light mode)
- **Slate 50:** `#f8fafc` — Background (dark mode text)

## Typography

### English
- **Display:** Inter (700-900 weight)
- **Body:** Inter (400-600 weight)

### Urdu
- **All:** Noto Nastaliq Urdu

## Voice & Tone
- **Friendly** — Like a helpful Pakistani shopkeeper friend
- **Bilingual** — Mix English + Roman Urdu naturally
- **Confident** — We know Pakistani business challenges
- **Modern** — Tech-savvy but accessible

## Usage Locations

### Web
- Marketing site: `apps/marketing/public/`
- Tenant web: `apps/web/public/`
- Admin panel: `apps/admin/public/`

### Mobile
- iOS/Android icon: `apps/mobile/assets/icon.png` (1024×1024)
- Android adaptive: `apps/mobile/assets/adaptive-icon.png` (512×512)
- Splash: `apps/mobile/assets/splash.png` (1284×2778)
- Notification: `apps/mobile/assets/notification-icon.png` (96×96, white silhouette)

### Email
- Header logo: 200×64 PNG, transparent background
- Use full-color version on white/light backgrounds

### Social Media
- Profile picture: Use `icon-512.png`
- Cover/banner: Use `og-image.png` (1200×630)
